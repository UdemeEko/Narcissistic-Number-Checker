What are Narcissistic Numbers?

They are numbers that are equal to the sum of their digits, each raised to the power of the total number of digits they have. They are also known as Armstrong Numbers.
For example:

  • 153 (3 digits): 13 + 53 + 33 = 1 + 125 + 27 = 153
  • 1634 (4 digits): 14 + 64 + 34 + 44 = 1 + 1296 + 81 + 256 = 1634
